AI Ping Pong Game





Instructions

The objective of the game is to not let the coomputer get 5 points. your job is to use your paddle to hit the green ball. If the ball touches the sides of the court, the computer gets one point. If the ball touches the top/bottom of the court, it will bounce off and keep playing. Move your mouse to control your paddle. As the game proggresses, the speed at which the ball is moving will increase. Also, if the ball crosses the speed of 13, and you miss, the computer wins.
